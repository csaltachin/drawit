welcome!

useful commands:

  • /name [nickname] | change nickname to [nickname]
  • /open [number] | opens room #[number]
  • /join [number] | joins room #[number]
  • /start [rounds] [draw-time] [choose-time] | start a game in current room, defaults: 2 rounds per player, 30 seconds for choosing and 90 seconds for drawing
  • /help | shows all commands
